This is example document showing that MSEA algorithm gives similiar results as standard EA for benchmark problem
defined as:

\begin{math}
max f(x, y) \\
f(x, y) = \sqrt[3]{x} sin(x) + \sqrt[3]{y} sin(y) + k\\
k \in \mathbb{R_{+}}
\end{math}

where k is any real number, used to make this function positive on the whole domain.
